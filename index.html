Chapter 1: The Harvest Season

In a field beneath the warm African sun, there lived a hardworking ant named Amani. Every day, she would gather grains and store them carefully in her underground home.

"The dry season will come," she would say, "and we must be prepared."

The Grasshopper's Song

Not far away, Gikuyu the grasshopper spent his days singing beautiful melodies. He watched Amani work but preferred to enjoy the moment.

"Why work so hard?" he called out. "The harvest is plentiful, and life is wonderful!"
